Electronic Arts have decided to [discontinue some of their earlier Xbox 360 games](http://news.cnet.com/8301-13506_3-20027365-17.html), meaning players will no longer be able to play online. With the 360 [still selling exceptionally well](http://technologizer.com/2011/01/14/2010-game-sales-microsoft/), the fact that games publishers are deprecating some of its older games must be worrying news for console owners. To think I can still play Team Fortress Classic, purchased in March 2000 to this day on my Windows 7 laptop - this amazing feat however is because the servers are not run by one company. [Lesson learnt?](http://www.overclockersclub.com/news/25732/)
