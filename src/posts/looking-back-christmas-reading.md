Christmas in 2020 will obviously be very different for many people, but like many people at Christmas I find myself looking back to my childhood, as let's be honest, that's when Christmas is at it's best isn't it?

On the topic of looking back, here are two fantastic articles from The Register that are by no means new, but worth a read or reread if you find yourself with some spare time over the lull between Christmas and New Year.

[Search engines we have known ... before Google crushed them](https://www.theregister.com/2012/12/18/search_engines_we_have_known/) - charts the history and demise of the early search engines. AltaVista, Lycos, Excite, GoTo, all the names you may remember from Christmas past.

[The inside story of the news website that saved the BBC](https://www.theregister.com/2012/11/28/the_bbc_news_online_story/) - A detailed look at how BBC News Online started. Back then nobody even knew what a news website should look like or how people would use it. They really were blazing the trail.
